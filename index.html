Achievements & Experience Highlights

Integration and Human–Technical Synergy

Within Mission Planning & Debriefing (MPDS) I aligned procedures, people and data, acting as the bridge among technical departments, pilots and management. Clear handovers, aircraft–simulator data consistency, and disciplined execution with a human-centred mindset for resource allocation, communication under pressure and decision quality.

RTMS Operations and Cross-Functional Leadership

Reference operator for the Real-Time Monitoring System (radar-like environment): acceptance tests and live operations, shoulder-to-shoulder with instructors and pilots. Translated complex system behaviour into actionable brief/debrief material, coordinating with stakeholders for smooth mission outcomes.

Global Ops Coverage

Airline, business and state/defence aviation, as employee and independent contractor. Planned and released operations worldwide including ETOPS, oceanic and remote airports, ensuring legal, meteorological and performance compliance for multiple airframes and mission types.

Diplomatic, Medevac, VVIP & ACMI/EACMI

Experience with medevac, VVIP movements (incl. Heads of State) and ACMI/EACMI. Rapid base set-up, short-notice rostering, and diplomatic coordination. Focus on slots/perms, robust alternates and cost-aware route optimisation.

EFB & Digital Cockpit

End-to-end cockpit data coordination: flight folder build, briefing package, document control, app currency, performance exports and distribution. Continuous crew feedback loop to keep cockpit data clear, consistent and current.

Core Dispatch Competencies

Meteorology (SIGWX, TAF/METAR trends, WAFS), NOTAM criticality assessment, AIRAC cycle validation, performance policy tuning, contingency/redispatch, drift/obstacle/limited scenarios, re-clear/redispatch logic when it benefits payload, time and engine-on-wing.

Performance & Tools

Aircraft performance profile build, testing and maintenance for planning platforms such as PPS (AIR SUPPORT) and local route-check tools. Local server supervision for data integrity/high availability and reliable exports to EFB and debrief environments.

Stakeholder Coordination

Daily liaison with ATC, handlers, CAM/part-145, medical providers and ground ops. Clear, time-bound comms balancing passenger care, medical urgency, safety margins and commercial constraints—keeping crews confident and decision-ready.
