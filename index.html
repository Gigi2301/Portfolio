About me

Aviation professional with a strong background in flight operations, mission systems and team leadership. Selected impact & experience highlights

Mission systems integration & human-technical bridge

Within Mission Planning & Debriefing (MPDS) I aligned procedures, people and data, acting as the bridge among technical departments, pilots and management. Clear handovers, aircraft–simulator data consistency and disciplined execution with a human-centred mindset, improving trust in data and reducing friction between teams.

RTMS & real-time mission support

Reference operator for the Real-Time Monitoring System (radar-like environment): acceptance tests and live operations, shoulder-to-shoulder with instructors and pilots. I turned complex system behaviour into brief/debrief material that crews could actually use, closing the loop between simulation, cockpit reality and training objectives.

Global flight operations coverage

Airline, business and state/defence aviation, as employee and independent contractor. Planned and released operations worldwide including ETOPS, oceanic and remote airports, ensuring legal, meteorological and performance compliance for multiple airframes and mission profiles, from scheduled flights to special missions.

Diplomatic, medevac, VVIP & ACMI/EACMI

Experience with medevac, VVIP movements (incl. Heads of State) and ACMI/EACMI. Rapid base set-up, short-notice rostering and diplomatic coordination. Focus on slots/permits, robust alternates and route choices that balance safety margins, medical urgency, cost and passenger experience.

EFB, digital cockpit & crew experience

End-to-end cockpit data coordination: flight folder build, briefing package, document control, app currency, performance exports and distribution. Continuous feedback from crews to keep cockpit information clear, consistent and easy to read in real-world conditions, not just on paper.

Dispatch & analytical core

Meteorology (SIGWX, TAF/METAR trends, WAFS), NOTAM criticality assessment, AIRAC cycle validation, performance policy tuning, contingency/redispatch and limited-scenario planning. Redispatch/re-clear logic when it genuinely benefits payload, time and engine-on-wing, not as a checkbox exercise.

Performance tools & data integrity

Aircraft performance profile build, testing and maintenance for planning platforms such as PPS (AIR SUPPORT) and local route-check tools. Local server supervision to keep data integrity and availability high, ensuring reliable exports towards EFB, simulators and debrief environments.

Stakeholder coordination & human factor

Daily liaison with ATC, handlers, CAM/part-145, medical providers and ground ops. Time-bound communication, balancing passenger care, medical or training priorities, safety margins and commercial constraints—helping crews stay confident and mentally free to focus on flying.
