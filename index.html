Achievements & Experience Highlights/h2>

Integration and Human–Technical Synergy

Within the Mission Planning & Debriefing environment, I focused on aligning procedures, people and data. I acted as a reference point between technical departments, pilots and management, ensuring clear handovers, consistent data across aircraft and simulator, and predictable mission outcomes. The approach blended operational discipline with a human-centered mindset for resource allocation, communication under pressure and decision clarity.

RTMS Operations and Cross-Functional Leadership

As the reference operator for the Real Time Monitoring System (RTMS) — a radar-like environment used to simulate airborne scenarios and adversary tracks — I supported both acceptance activities and live operations. I worked alongside instructors and pilots, monitoring scenarios, troubleshooting issues and translating complex system behavior into actionable brief/debrief insights. This strengthened my ability to coordinate multidisciplinary workflows and make technical information usable for different audiences.

Global Operations

Experience across scheduled airlines and business aviation, both as staff and independent contractor. Planned and released worldwide routes including ETOPS sectors, oceanic and remote operations, ensuring legal, meteorological and performance compliance for diverse fleets and mission types.

Special Missions & Profiles

Hands-on dispatch support for MedEvac (time-critical medical evacuations), Diplomatic/VVIP movements (incl. Heads of State), and ACMI/EACMI operations with rapid-base setup, short notice repositioning and cross-authority coordination. Focus on slot/permit strategy, alternates resilience and crew duty optimization.

EFB & Digital Ops

End-to-end management of EFB ecosystem: flight folder build, briefing packages, document control, app currency, performance exports and distribution. Continuous feedback loop with crews to keep cockpit data clear, consistent and current.

Core Dispatch Competencies

Integrated analysis of Meteorology (SIGWX, TAF/METAR trends, WAFS models), NOTAM criticality assessment, AIRAC cycle updates and route validation. Fuel policy tailoring (contingency/extra/isolated), drift-down and obstacle-limited scenarios, reclear and redispatch logic when beneficial to payload and time-on-wing.

Performance & Infrastructure

Aircraft performance profile creation, testing and maintenance for planning platforms such as PPS (AIR SUPPORT), including cost index policy and en-route performance checks. Local server supervision for data integrity and availability, ensuring reliable exports to EFB and debrief environments.

Stakeholder Coordination

Daily liaison with ATC, handlers, CAA/permit desks, medical providers and ground ops. Clear, time-bound comms to balance passenger care, medical urgency, safety margins and commercial constraints—keeping crews confident and decision-ready.
